Entering Electricity Data
If you have electricity sources there are two ways of entering the data. This is done manually or by uploading a file containing the data (generally a csv file). If you just have a single value of how much electricity you have used for each month you should enter your data manually. If you have a list of hourly meter readings provided by your utility company or by a smart meter than you should upload a file.
Manual Entry of Electricity Data
This guide will show you how to upload electricity data manually, we use the terminology electricity reading to refer to a single piece of data which states how much electricity was used between two dates.

To enter electricity data manually click on the menu Upload > Enter Electricity Data
Fig.12 Manual Entry of Electricity Data

Next click on the Enter New Electricity Reading button

Fig.13 Enter New Electricity Reading

TThis will bring you to the form to enter your Electricity Readings

Fig.14 Electricity Readings Form
  • Electricity Value: This is the amount of electricity that was used during your billing period. You will find this on your electricity bill.
  • Start Date: Select from the date picker the first day your electricity reading refers to. This would be the date on your electricity bill when the billing period starts.
  • End Date: Select from the date picker the last day your electricity reading refers to. This would be the date on your electricity bill when the billing period ends.
  • Units: This is the units that your electricity usage is measure in. This will be stated on your electricity bill.
  • Meter: Select the meter that this reading is coming from. If this list is empty you need to refer to the Configuring Meters section.

Once you have filled everything out click on the Save button.

This will bring you back to the page listing all electricity readings which have been created with a message stating the successful creation of your electricity reading.
