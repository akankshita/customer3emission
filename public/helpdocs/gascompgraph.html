Analysing the Gas Comparisons Graph

The Gas Comparisons graph is the second option in the tabs and can also be located by clicking Graphs > Gas Totals

These graphs allow the user to compare their organisations gas usage to highlight abnormalities. There are 7 different comparisons graphs, allowing you to compare gas usage using different parameters.

There is also an option to choose a time period for which the data in compared. Fig.38 below depicts the 7 different comparison graphs and the time period option.

Fig.38 Different Comparisons Graphs

Hovering over any of the 7 comparison graph filter will display the graph. As you can see in the above image, the first comparison graph depicts "Emissions By Month".
