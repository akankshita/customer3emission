Analysing the Gas Totals Graph

The Gas Total graph operates in the exact same manner as the Status graph and the Electricity Usage graph.

There are 2 main differences however

  • These filters do not include a time resolution filter option as gas is constantly pumped through a gas pipeline and not at different frequencies.
  • To learn how to use the Gas Usage graph and filters refer to the section above (Analyzing the Electricity Totals Graph).
