Graph Analysis

This section shows you how to use the graphs in the application to analyze your organisations emissions and energy usage. It also shows you how filter specific data on graphs, compare the data, print the graphs and attach them to email. The graphs can all be viewed by either clicking the Home button in the main menu. The graphs will appear under the 5 tabs on the home page with the first being the Status tab.
