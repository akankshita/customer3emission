The Status Page
This is the "landing page" of the application, where you will be taken every time you log into the e-MissionManager. The status home page gives you an overall status of your organisations emissions, status of your reporting requirements, a CRC related news feed and administration details of the last user to use the application.
Fig.1 The Status Page

The Status page consists of the following information.

  • Tabs: Allow you to view different aspects of your emission monitoring process.
  • Emissions Graph: A graph depicting your organisations emissions (in CO2 equivalence). Clicking and Dragging horizontally on the graph will allow you to zoom in and view a more accurate illustration of your data. Once this is done a "reset zoom" link will appear in graph allowing you to reset to the default view.
  • Reporting Task Status: 3 icons showing how many of the tasks are complete/ongoing/incomplete. These relate only to registered participants of the Carbon Reduction Commitment (CRC) scheme in the United Kingdom. The first two icon are the required reports and the third icon displays how many documents are saved to your evidence pack.
  • CRC Related News Feed: This news feed displays up-to-date information concerning the scheme. It is intended to keep all users alert to changes within the scheme or other CRC related issues. Clicking on any of the listed items in the news feed will take you directly to the source of the article.
  • User Log: The user log at the bottom of this page displays the name, date, time and ip address of the last user to log into the system.
